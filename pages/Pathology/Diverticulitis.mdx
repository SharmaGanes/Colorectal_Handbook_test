# Diverticulitis

<details>
  <summary>Definition</summary>
  Pockets in the lining of the intestine become inflammed. Clinical Picture: Elderly
  patient with previous history of constipation.
</details>

<details>
  <summary>Clinical Features</summary>
  Abdominal Pain: LIF Localised Peritonitis Pyrexia
</details>

<details>
  <summary>Investigations</summary>
  1️⃣ Bloods: Raised WCC; Raised CRP; Amylase; G+S/X match 2️⃣ Imaging: Erect CXR (Perforation);
  AXR (Fluid level); Contrast CT 3️⃣ Endoscopy: Flexi Sig; ❗️ Colonoscopy is CI in
  acute attack
</details>

<details>
  <summary>Management: Medical</summary>
  1️⃣ NBM; IV Fluids; Analgesia; Abx: Cefuroxime + Metronidazole; Most cases settle
</details>

<details>
  <summary>Management: Surgical</summary>
  1️⃣ Hartmann's: To resect dead bowel
</details>
