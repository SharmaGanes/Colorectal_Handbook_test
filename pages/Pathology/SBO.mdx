# Small Bowel Obstruction

<details>
  <summary>Causes</summary>
  **adhesions (pelvic surgery)** > tumor > hernia (inguinal)
</details>

<details>
  <summary>Clinical Features</summary>
  Proximal Obstruction: Bilious Vomitting; Distal Obstruction: Feculent Obstruction;
  High Pitched Bowel Sounds
</details>

<details>
  <summary>Investigations</summary>
  Abdominal X-ray: dilated bowel, air-fluid levels, “stack of coins”; Gold Standard:
  CT Abdomen
</details>

<details>
  <summary>Management</summary>
  NGT and Surgery
</details>
